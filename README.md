Project Overview :-

Apple Retail Analytics is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze retail store performance, sales trends, customer behavior, and key business metrics. The dashboard transforms raw retail data into actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making.

Objectives :-

- Monitor overall sales performance across Apple retail stores.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Analyze revenue trends and customer purchasing patterns.
- Track key performance indicators (KPIs) for business growth.
- Enable stakeholders to make informed strategic decisions.

Tools & Technologies :-

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Microsoft Excel

Key Metrics :-

- Total Sales
- Total Revenue
- Units Sold
- Average Sales per Store
- Product Performance
- Regional Performance

Dashboard Features :-

- Interactive filters and slicers
- KPI cards for quick performance tracking
- Sales trend analysis
- Product category analysis
- Regional sales comparison
- Dynamic visualizations for business insights

Business Insights :-

- Identified top-performing products contributing the highest revenue.
- Compared sales performance across different regions and stores.
- Analyzed sales trends to highlight growth opportunities.
- Enabled quick identification of high and low-performing segments.
